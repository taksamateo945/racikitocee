Fans are still holding out hope that Taylor Kinney and Lady Gaga will get back together. Will Gaga be Taylor Kinney’s wife? Has he had a girlfriend after the breakup? Find out! Also, know his net worth in 2018.

Taylor Kinney wife: Getting back together with Lady Gaga?

Kinney began dating Lady Gaga in 2011 after meeting her on the set of her music video for You and I. He proposed to her on February 14, 2015. Shockingly, the couple called it quits in July 2016. Fans and their families were quite upset when the news broke out and hoped they would get back together soon.

Even Gaga hopes that they will find their way back to each other after some time. “Taylor and I have always believed we are soul mates. Just like all couples, we have ups and downs, and we have been taking a break. We are both ambitious artists, hoping to work through long-distance and complicated schedules to continue the simple love we have always shared,” wrote the singer on Instagram.

Kinney reportedly moved on with a waitress named Alanna DiGiovanni in October 2016. The 36-year-old is presently single. Maybe there’s still hope for the couple!

Kinney opened up about his dating life for the first time after the split in March 2017. In an interview with Entertainment Tonight, he joked, “My dating life is through Chicago Fire. I live vicariously through my character.”

Taylor Kinney net worth 2018

Kinney had made his acting debut in 2006, playing Luke Gianni, in the television series Fashion House. His role in the CW series The Vampire Diaries helped him get noticed. He began starring in Chicago Fire in 2012 and appeared in Zero Dark Thirty the same year.

He has also starred in Chicago P.D. and Chicago Med. In 2018, he will be starring in the movie Best Day of My Life with Sarah Jessica Parker. His net worth is estimated to be $8 million.
