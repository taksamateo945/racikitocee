Haha Memes

Laughter is a pleasant physical relation usually consisting of rhythmical, often audible contractions of the diaphragm and other respiratory system parts. It is a part of human behavior regulated by the brain, helping humans clarify their intentions in social interaction and providing an emotional context to conversations. Laughter is used to signaling that being part of a group-it signals acceptance and positive interactions with others. Laughter is sometimes seen as contagious, and the laughter of one person can itself provoke laughter from others as positive feedback.

It is fun to share a good laugh, but it can also improve our health. So, it is strong medicine. It strengthens our immune system, boosts mood, diminishes pain, and protects us from the damaging effects of stress.

Haha is an onomatopoeic representation of laughter. So, most commonly, it is considered an auditory expression of several positive emotional states, such as joy, delight, happiness, or relief.

Haha is an expression that is often featured in reaction images and animated GIFs to dismiss someone else’s opinion condescendingly. However, haha also means you are genuinely amused and that maybe you laughed a little in real life.

With the wide use of social media, we express our amusement and share the laughter has also become digital. This must be the reason that this year’s most popular emoji was “the tears of joy.” However, as much as everyone is saying haha and LOL online, very few are laughing in real life. Expressions of laughter online don’t necessarily mean something is funny. It could be a sarcastic laugh in response to a terrible joke.
