Luke Perry Would Rather Marry Tori Spelling Than Jennie Garth or Shannen Doherty

February 08, 2024
blog

If Luke Perry were forced to choose between Brenda Walsh and Kelly Taylor today, the Beverly Hills, 90210 alum would have no problem making a decision: "Kelly," he said during an appearance on Bravo's Watch What Happens Live Jan. 22. "Because she has more money!"

The actor, who played bad boy Dylan McKay on the '90s teen soap, gamely agreed to play a round of "Marry, Shag, Kill" with WWHL host Andy Cohen. He was then presented with three options: former costars Tori Spelling, Shannen Doherty and Jennie Garth."I'd marry Tori because I told her dad I'd look out for her," Perry said of the actress' late father, TV producer Aaron Spelling, who passed away in 2006 at age 83. "That's the best way for me to do that. I'd kill Shannon, because she'd want me to. And that's the only reason. And what was the other one?"

"Shag," Cohen reminded him.

"Well, there you go!" the 47-year-old said of Garth, now dating rocker Jeremy Salken following her March 2011 split from husband Peter Facinelli. (Perry later called the 40-year-old a "good kisser," while Doherty, 41, "smelled good.")
