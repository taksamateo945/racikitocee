Gwyneth Paltrow’s daughter just turned 16 years old and she already looks all grown up! The actress took to social media to pay tribute to Apple and also share some new pics of her!

The mom could not believe how fast kids these days are growing up, her daughter included!

Of course, for her it’s quite shocking but Apple is no longer a child, she is right in the middle of her teenage years!

In the caption, the proud mother still made sure to celebrate the young woman her daughter is turning into.

‘I can’t believe I am actually writing these words but…happy sweet 16 my darling girl. You’re the light of my heart, you are pure joy. You’re wickedly intelligent and have the best, most dry and most brilliant sense of humor. I have the best time just being your mom. I love our nightly chats when I really get to hear what is on your mind.’

She went on to gush over her some more in the lengthy caption, writing: ‘You work hard to get whatever it is that you want to achieve, and you have grit and responsibility in spades. I’m so damn lucky to be your mother, you beautiful, and kind young woman. Thank you for choosing me. I adore you to the moon and back a zillion times. I am sorry you are having this particular birthday during these circumstances, but as always with you, you find the best in everything.’

The Goop star has been in quarantine just like everyone as well but working from home has been much easier thanks to the ‘moral support’ she’s been getting from her kids – Apple, and her brother Moses – who she shares with Chris Martin.
