Gavin Rossdale Moves Into Bachelor Pad After Gwen Stefani Split

March 02, 2024
blog

The sweet escape? Gavin Rossdale has packed his bags following his split from Gwen Stefani. The Bush frontman recently moved into a new bachelor pad, Us Weekly can confirm.

A source tells Us that Rossdale, 49, is now living in a $6.5 million house in L.A.'s Bel-Air neighborhood. He's been slowly unpacking and has hit up furniture stores like Cassina, Diva Group, Ligne Roset, and Roche Bobois to adorn the place.

"He’s picked out a lot of stuff and is enjoying the decorating process," the insider says. "He carries around a notebook and takes pictures of the stuff he likes." 

Last month, the estranged couple confirmed to Us that they had amicably decided to end their 13-year marriage. They share sons Kingston, 9, Zuma, 6, and Apollo, 17 months.

"She isn't angry with Gavin. But a year ago, he was already so removed from the marriage and Gwen," a second source previously told Us. "The split has been in the works for a while and during all their bad times it made Gwen more focused on work."
